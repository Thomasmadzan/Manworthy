Air Sea Cargo- The Company

Air Sea Cargo is a Customs Clearing, Forwarding and International Trade Consultancy licensed Company, currently operating in Zimbabwe.

Our strength lies in our management’s experience and passion in adding value to organizational supply chain.

AIR SEA CARGO OPERATIONS





MANAGEMENT

Daniel Mhloro

Daniel Mhloro is the Managing Director (MD) of Air Sea Cargo. Daniel is a former Department of Customs and Excise Official, and has more 20 years’ experience having worked in various capacities at Cater craft (PVT) Ltd and Manica Freight. Daniel attended and passed the Customs Examining Officers course. He holds an Honors degree in Bachelor of Commerce in Transport and Logistics and other qualifications including Imports procedures, Advanced Customs Clearing Course and Elementary Cargo Handling course.



Sithabile Mhloro

Sithabile Mhloro is the Operations Director. Sithabile has more than 7 years’ experience in shipping being a former employee of Cargo Services as a Shipping Clerk.

Operations Resources

In addition to the above directors, the company employs an administrator / Accounts Personnel and experienced clerks who handle customs clearing and forwarding issues at both Harare and Beitbridge offices.
